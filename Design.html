KITCHEN REMODELING



Roeser Home Remodeling specializes in kitchen remodeling in St. Louis. We’ve been renovating outdated kitchens for more than 40 years. Whether your kitchen needs a complete remodel or just a facelift, call us. Our design-build team of experts will transform your cramped, old, or poorly designed kitchen into a space you will love to spend time in. The architects and designers at Roeser Home Remodeling will work with you to give your kitchen remodel project the quality of service it deserves. We are authorized installers for Wellborn Cabinets. BATHROOM REMODELING



We are experts in bathroom remodeling. We’ve been updating bathrooms in Kirkwood homes since 1977. Many older homes have just one bathroom and their layouts are outdated. We can build another for your home or design a bathroom renovation so the one you have works for you. Modern families have far different needs and wants than those back in the 1960s or earlier. And a lot of progress has been made on Universal Design principles so people can age in place. Whether your home needs a complete bathroom renovation or just a refresh with new cabinetry or hardware, we can help. Our satisfied clients’ testimonials are proof that we are one of the most trusted bathroom remodelers in the St. Louis area.

BASEMENT REMODELING



If you’re thinking of doing a basement renovation, you need a home remodeling professional. There are too many potential problems to leave the job to a fly-by-night company. Roeser Home Remodeling has been in business since 1977. Our team of experts will take care of the details of a basement remodel that you may not think about – the water table level, egress regulations and building around problems such as shifting foundations. The design-build team at Roeser Home Remodeling can transform your dark, dank lower level and turn it into a basement with such amenities as a game room, wine cellar or home theater. Don’t take our word for it. Read testimonials and reviews from some our satisfied clients in the St. Louis metro area.

HOME ADDITIONS



Sometimes you need more than a kitchen remodeled or a bathroom renovated. You need an addition built onto your home. For more than 40 years, Roeser Home Remodeling has been helping homeowners create space for changes in their lives. Whether it’s the start of a home-based business or a growing family, our design-build team can help you get the space you need. A home addition can be anything from a bump-out, a room built on the side or an expansion from the top floor. If you’re not sure what you need, contact us. Our design-build team of experts will talk to you about your options. Whatever you decide, you will get quality craftsmanship and a look that blends with the rest of your home’s exterior.
